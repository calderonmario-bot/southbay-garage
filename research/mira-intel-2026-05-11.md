## Executive Summary

Our $799 flat-rate positioning is competitive but sits at the higher end for smaller garage cleanouts. The opportunity lies in our transparency and simplicity—competitors rely on confusing volume-based pricing that leaves customers uncertain until the truck arrives.

## 2. Pricing Gap Analysis

### Our Position: $799 Flat Rate (Any Size Garage)

| Scenario | Our Price | Competitor Range | Position |
|----------|-----------|------------------|----------|
| Small garage (1/4 truck) | $799 | $99 - $225 | Higher than market |
| Medium garage (1/2 truck) | $799 | $199 - $475 | Mid-to-high |
| Large garage (full truck) | $799 | $399 - $658 | Competitive/Good |
| Oversized (more than truck) | $799 | $700+ | Excellent value |

**Strategic Insight:**
- We win on large garages and complex cleanouts
- We lose on small single-item or 1/4-truck jobs
- **Recommendation:** Consider positioning $799 as "up to a full truckload"—makes the value clear vs. Junk King's $589-$658 for same volume

## 3. Strategic Positioning Opportunities

### Competitor Weaknesses to Exploit:

1. **Price Uncertainty**
   - All major competitors require on-site estimates
   - Customers hate surprises
   - **Our angle:** "$799. That's it. No estimates. No surprises."

2. **Hidden Fees Risk**
   - Fridges, A/C units, paint, tires often have surcharges
   - Heavy/bulky items cost extra
   - **Our angle:** "Any size garage. Any items included."

3. **Volume Confusion**
   - 1/8th truck? 1/4? Customers can't visualize
   - **Our angle:** "You don't measure your garage in truckloads. Neither do we."

4. **No Same-Day Guarantee**
   - Junk Trash Removal charges 10% extra for same-day
   - After-hours: 50% premium
   - **Our angle:** Same-day availability as standard (when possible)

## 7. Pricing Objection Scripts for Kai (Sales)

### Objection: "That's more expensive than [competitor]."

**Response Framework:**
> "I totally get it. Here's what most people don't realize: that $199 quote from [competitor] is for 1/8 of their truck. Most garages need at least half. Their half-truck price is $400-$500, and that's before fees for heavy items, stairs, or same-day service.
>
> Our $799 is any size garage, any items, no surprises. The price you hear is the price you pay. Want me to walk you through what's actually in your garage so you can compare apples to apples?"

### Objection: "Can you do it for less?"

**Response Framework:**
> "I wish I could. The $799 covers the truck, the crew, disposal fees, and gets you your garage back in one day. If the job's smaller than expected, we still charge $799—it keeps our pricing simple and our crew paid fairly.
>
> What I can offer is [same-day/next-day availability / flexible timing / helping you sort donate items]. What matters most to you—speed, or are you still comparing options?"
