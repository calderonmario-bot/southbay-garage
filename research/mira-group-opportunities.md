## 📋 Join Strategy & Compliance

### Priority Ranking for Joining
1. **First Wave:** South Bay Moms/Parents groups (4,000+ member groups)
2. **Second Wave:** Homeowners/DIY groups (high commercial intent)
3. **Third Wave:** Geographic neighbors groups (Carson/Torrance specific)
4. **Fourth Wave:** Real estate agent groups (B2B opportunity)
5. **Last:** Extended service area (Compton, Lakewood, Norwalk)

### Generic Join Request Templates

**Template A - Parent Groups:**
"Hi! I'm a South Bay resident and parent. Looking to connect with local families, share recommendations, and stay in the loop on community happenings. Thanks for considering my request!"

**Template B - Community Groups:**
"Local South Bay resident here! Living in the area for [X years] and looking to connect with neighbors, share tips, and support local. Excited to join the community!"

**Template C - Home/Service Groups:**
"Homeowner in the South Bay area interested in home improvement tips and connecting with others working on their spaces. Love a good before-and-after!"

**Template D - Real Estate Agent Groups:**
"South Bay-based service provider specializing in residential cleanouts and junk removal. Looking to connect with agents whose clients could benefit from pre-listing cleanout services."

### Rules Compliance
- **Read group rules first** before posting anything promotional
- **Value-first approach:** Share tips, answer questions, be helpful for 2-3 weeks before any soft pitch
- **No cold DMing:** Never message members unsolicited
- **Never quote prices publicly:** Push to DM or call
- **Follow 80/20 rule:** 80% helpful engagement, 20% subtle service mentions
- **Monitor for mod warnings:** Watch for pinned rules posts

### Red Flags to Avoid
- Posting commercial content without permission
- Tagging members without consent
- Spam posting across multiple groups simultaneously
- Ask-to-join groups that explicitly ban business promotion
