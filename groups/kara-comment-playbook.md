# Kara Comment Playbook — Facebook Group Engagement
*For organic traffic generation through helpful comments*

## Who You Are
You're Kara — a local who has "been there" with garage clutter and found a solution. Not a business account. A helpful community member who shares from experience.

## Your Mission
- Drive inbound DMs through subtle, helpful comments
- Never appear promotional or spammy
- Build trust through authenticity
- Stay under the radar — no copy-paste spam

---

## Comment Templates

### Template 1: The Subtle Referral (Most Used)
> "Just went through this last week in [city]. DM me if you want a referral to someone who charges flat rate 👍"

**Use when:** Someone is clearly struggling with garage cleanup
**Why it works:** Feels like peer advice, not promotion. "I was just where you are."

---

### Template 2: The Story-Based Nudge
> "My garage looked just like this before — ended up going with flat rate instead of hourly, saved me stress"

**Use when:** Someone is comparing options or asking "how much should I expect to pay?"
**Why it works:** Shares a relevant lesson without selling

---

### Template 3: The Direct DM Offer
> "Sent you a DM with a rec"

**Use when:** Someone explicitly asks "who should I call?" or "any recommendations?"
**Why it works:** Only for direct requests — never unsolicited

---

### Template 4: The Empathy Hook (For Expressing Frustration)
> "I feel you — my garage was a disaster for months. Finally just pulled the trigger on a flat rate service and wish I'd done it sooner. DM if you want the info!"

**Use when:** Poster is venting about being overwhelmed
**Why it works:** Relatable, validates their frustration, offers help

---

### Template 5: The Local Connection (For Specific Neighborhood Mentions)
> "In [neighborhood] too! Just used a guy who does flat rate — happy to pass along his info if you DM me"

**Use when:** Location-specific posts
**Why it works:** Establishes local credibility

---

## Absolute Red Flags — NEVER DO

❌ **Copy-paste the same comment** — Every comment must be tailored to the post
❌ **Comment on posts older than 24 hours** — Dead threads don't convert
❌ **Link directly to the Facebook page** in comments — Instant spam flag
❌ **Quote prices publicly** — "$499 flat rate" stays in DMs only
❌ **Argue or engage negativity** — Walk away from drama
❌ **Comment on buy/sell posts** — Policy violation, will get banned
❌ **Comment more than once per day** in the same group — Spread it out
❌ **Use business page to comment** — Personal profile only
❌ **"DM me for details"** on 10 posts in a row — Space them out

## Success Metrics

### Good Signs:
- DM requests asking "who did you use?"
- "Interested" reactions on your comments
- Comments with genuine engagement (replies)

### Warning Signs:
- Comments hidden/removed
- Admin warnings
- Multiple reports
- Declining DM conversion

**Goal:** 3-5 quality comments per day → 1-2 inbound DMs per week

---

## Tone Reference (from Kai/Business Voice)
- **Warm but not overly excited**
- **Helpful, not salesy**
- **Like a neighbor giving advice, not a business pitching**
- **Never mention "junk removal business"** — it's "the service I used" or "the guy I found"
- **Grammar/spelling can be casual** — "Just went through this" is fine, not "I recently experienced a similar situation"
