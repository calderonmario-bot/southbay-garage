## 2. Algorithm & Timing Optimization

### Best Times to Comment (2024 Algorithm)

**Facebook's "Most Relevant" comment ranking means timing matters MORE than ever.**

#### Peak Visibility Windows:

| Day | Best Time | Why |
|-----|-----------|-----|
| Tuesday–Thursday | 10 AM – 3 PM | General Facebook peak engagement |
| Tuesday–Thursday | 1 PM (lunch) | Midday surge — highest activity |
| Weekends | 7–9 PM | Local/community group activity peaks |

#### The "Golden Hour" Rule:
**Comment within the FIRST 2 HOURS** of a post being published for maximum visibility.
- Posts older than 4 hours lose 60%+ visibility in active groups
- The algorithm prioritizes "fresh" engagement
- Early comments get more reply/action exposure

#### South Bay-Specific Timing:
- **Morning commuters:** 7–9 AM (checking phones on transit)
- **Lunch scroll:** 12–1:30 PM
- **Evening wind-down:** 8–10 PM (parents planning next day)

## 5. Groups to AVOID (Red Flags)

### High-Risk Group Types:

| Red Flag | Why | Consequence |
|----------|-----|-------------|
| "No self-promotion" in rules | Easy ban | Wasted time, account strike |
| Aggressive moderation | "Spam" trigger-happy | Comments deleted, warnings |
| Oversaturated with competitors | 10+ junk removal comments per post | No visibility, looks spammy |
| Buy/sell only groups | Policy violation | Immediate removal |
| Huge groups (>50k) | Noise too high | Low conversion, high risk |
| Inactive groups | <3 posts/day | Dead audience |

### Specific Warning Signs:
- Multiple pinned "NO PROMOTION" posts
- Admin comments like "stop spamming" on posts
- Same 3–4 competitors commenting on every post
- Low engagement (posts with 0–2 comments)
- Groups where members report each other frequently

## 6. This Week's 5 High-Opportunity Post Types to Watch For

### Post Type #1: "Overwhelmed" Garage Posts
**Keywords:** "can't even," "don't know where to start," "years of stuff"
**Template:** Empathy Hook (#4)
**Timing:** Within 1 hour of posting
**Why it converts:** Emotional buying trigger

### Post Type #2: Moving + Time Pressure
**Keywords:** "moving next week," "need gone by," "closing soon"
**Template:** Subtle Referral (#1)
**Timing:** IMMEDIATELY (time-sensitive)
**Why it converts:** Urgency = faster decisions

### Post Type #3: Estate/Loss Situations
**Keywords:** "inherited," "passed away," "parents' house," "need to clear out"
**Template:** Story-Based Nudge (#2) + empathy
**Timing:** Within 2 hours
**Why it converts:** Complex situation, needs trusted help

### Post Type #4: "Who should I call?" Direct Asks
**Keywords:** "any recommendations?", "who does this?", "looking for someone"
**Template:** Direct DM Offer (#3)
**Timing:** Within 30 minutes
**Why it converts:** They're literally asking for you

### Post Type #5: DIY Failure Posts
**Keywords:** "tried to clean," "thought I could," "gave up"
**Template:** Empathy Hook (#4) + "wish I'd done it sooner"
**Timing:** Within 1 hour
**Why it converts:** Proof they can't DIY, ready to pay
