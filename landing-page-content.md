- **Mobile-first:** Carrd defaults to mobile-responsive
- **Spacing:** Generous padding between sections (60-80px)
- **Buttons:** Bold, high contrast, rounded corners (8px radius)
- **Images:** Garage photos only, no people stock photos

## Page Flow Summary

1. **Hook** (Hero) → grabs attention with clear value prop
2. **Agitate** (Problem) → makes reader feel understood
3. **Solve** (Solution) → shows exactly what's included
4. **Prove** (Before/After) → visual credibility
5. **Assure** (Service Area) → confirms you serve them
6. **Convert** (Contact) → clear action to take
