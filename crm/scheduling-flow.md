## Step 2: Pre-Call Confirmation Checklist

Once appointment is scheduled, confirm these details:

| Item | Why It Matters |
|------|----------------|
| **Full address** | Verify service area, calculate drive time |
| **Garage access** | Is it standalone? Attached? Storage unit? |
| **Parking for truck** | Need ~20ft of curb space |
| **Gate codes / access issues** | Save time on arrival |
| **Payment method** | Card on file? Invoice? Cash? |
| **Photos of garage** | Set crew expectations |
