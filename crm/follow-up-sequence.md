**Purpose:** Systematic follow-up that converts quoted leads into booked appointments
**Success Metric:** Lead → scheduled appointment rate
**Updated:** 2026-05-11

---

## Sequence Overview

```
Hour 0 ──▶ Day 1 ──▶ Day 3 ──▶ Day 7 ──▶ Day 14 ──▶ Archive
 (ack)     (quote)   (nudge)   (final)   (last try)
```

| Touchpoint | Timing | Method | Goal |
|------------|--------|--------|------|
| **Immediate** | 0-1 hour | Text/DM | Acknowledge, build trust |
| **Day 1** | 24 hours | Text/Email | Confirm quote, push scheduling |
| **Day 3** | 72 hours | Text | Gentle check-in |
| **Day 7** | 1 week | Text/Call | Final follow-up, urgency |
| **Day 14** | 2 weeks | Email | Archive or retain for nurture |

## Response Handling

| Lead Response | Action | Status Change |
|---------------|--------|---------------|
| "Yes, let's book" | Send Calendly or propose 2-3 times | NEW → SCHEDULED |
| "I have questions" | Answer, then push to schedule | QUALIFIED |
| "Need to think about it" | Set Day 7 follow-up | QUOTED |
| "Too expensive" | Offer payment plan or counter if appropriate | QUOTED / NO BOOK |
| "Not right now" | Set future reminder (30/60/90 days) | NO BOOK |
| "Hired someone else" | Archive, note competitor | NO BOOK |
| No response after Day 14 | Archive (cold lead) | NO BOOK → ARCHIVED |

---

## 🔥 HOT LEAD Flags

Any lead showing these signs → Fast-track scheduling:

- Says "this week" or "ASAP" for timing
- Asks about weekend availability
- Sends photos unprompted
- Mentions moving, listing house, or event deadline
- Replies within minutes to initial message

**Action for HOT leads:** Skip Day 1/3 delays, book immediately
